Biography

I am a recognized industry leader with more than 20 years in leading Research & Development projects. I started in wireless communications in the 90s and in 2000’s started diversifying into Machine Learning initially for wireless networks and later for all sorts of applications. Currently leading ~15 researchers in Murray Hill NJ and Budapest in Applied Machine Learning working on real time multisensory data fusion applications. 15 journal publications and 50 patents issued/pending. As of spring 2019 I am also with the Computer Science department of NJIT.
